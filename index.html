About Maxwell

A Son of Kabiro,
Rising for the People

Born and raised in Kabiro, Maxwell Changilwa Amugune knows every street, every struggle, and every family firsthand. His mission is clear: to Reset, Restore, and Rebuild Kabiro — without political brokers, without middlemen. Just direct, honest service to the residents of Dagoretti North.

From advocating for mjengo workers' rights to walking every rough road in the ward, Maxwell is not a stranger who promises — he is a neighbor who delivers.

🏗️ Mjengo Community

The family that made me who I am. I will never stop advocating for the rights of all site workers. Every mjengo worker in Kabiro deserves dignity, fair pay, and protection.

🛡️ Security

We don't just need street lights — we need security personnel on the ground in Kabiro.

  • 2 operational police posts in Kabiro to end theft
  • Pledge to make Kabiro streets safe for every resident

🏥 Healthcare

A dispensary with a fully equipped emergency response ambulance. In one meeting I nearly died from food poisoning — all hospitals were closed on a Sunday. How many die daily due to such?

  • Subsidized medication for all residents
  • 24/7 emergency ambulance
  • Fully staffed dispensary in Kabiro

♻️ Sanitation — TakaSafi TakaKazi

Vijana wapewe kazi, mtaa ingare. Proper waste management that creates jobs for Kabiro youth.

  • Youth employment through sanitation contracts
  • Solutions that don't damage our roads
